Subprocessors

Last updated: 12 June 2026

To operate the Service, SKYLINE INTELLIGENCE engages third-party vendors that may process personal data on our behalf ("subprocessors"). Each subprocessor is bound by a data-processing agreement that limits use to the purposes we specify.

This page lists the subprocessors in use today and explains how we notify you when that list changes.

Notice We notify all account holders by email at least 14 days before a new subprocessor begins processing your data, giving you an opportunity to object. Subscribe below to receive these notices directly.

1. Infrastructure & platform

Cloud hosting, storage, and observability
Subprocessor Purpose Region
Amazon Web Services Cloud hosting, compute, and object storage for SKYLINE platform services and customer request logs. US, EU
Cloudflare Edge networking, DDoS protection, and TLS termination. Global edge
PostgreSQL hosting tier
(via managed Postgres provider)		 Primary database hosting for user accounts, credit balances, and usage records. US, EU

2. Payments

Payment processing and tax
Subprocessor Purpose Region
Stripe Payment processing for credit purchases and auto top-up. Stripe receives billing details and tokenizes payment methods; we store only tokenized references. US, EU
TaxJar Sales-tax calculation for US transactions. US

3. Communications

Transactional and support messaging
Subprocessor Purpose Region
Postmark Transactional email: account verification, security alerts, subprocessor-change notices, and billing receipts. US
Intercom Customer support chat and help-center content. Conversations may be reviewed by our support team. US, EU

4. Observability & security

Error tracking, logs, and uptime monitoring
Subprocessor Purpose Region
Sentry Application error tracking and stack-trace aggregation. Prompt and completion content is excluded by default. US
Datadog Infrastructure metrics, uptime monitoring, and security log aggregation. US, EU

5. Model Providers (separate disclosure)

Model Providers are not subprocessors in the infrastructure sense — they process your prompts as independent data controllers, not on our behalf. They are listed separately at skylineintelligence.top/models and are subject to their own privacy policies.
